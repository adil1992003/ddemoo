WHAT IS SUPER BIKE

      A superbike is a high-performance motorcycle characterized by its powerful engine, advanced technology, and sporty design.
It's a term often used to describe motorcycles built for both street and track use,
offering a thrilling riding experience with a focus on speed, acceleration, and responsive handling

1.kawasaki ninja h2

The Kawasaki Ninja H2 is a hyper sport motorcycle known for
its supercharged engine and high performance.
Key features include a 998cc inline-four engine with a supercharger,
producing significant power
(around 231 PS or 200PS depending on the model and tune),
and a high top speed (approaching 200 mph or 320 km/h).

  • Engine: 998cc inline-4
  • Power: ~203 hp
  • Top Speed: 300 km/h
  • Price: ₹16–17 lakh
  • Highlights: Superb value-for-money superbike with
    race-proven tech and strong community support.

2.bmw s1000 rr

The BMW S1000RR boasts a powerful engine, advanced electronics,
and aerodynamic features. Key highlights include a 999cc inline-4 engine,
Dynamic Traction Control, multiple riding modes, and an optional M package with
carbon fiber components and enhanced aerodynamics

  • Engine: 999cc inline-4
  • Power: ~210 hp
  • Top Speed: 299+ km/h
  • Price: ₹21–27 lakh
  • Highlights: Asymmetrical headlamps (earlier models),
    ShiftCam tech, excellent electronics package.

3.ducati penigale v4

The fastest Ducati motorcycle is the Panigale V4 R.
It's considered one of the fastest production bikes on the planet,
with a focus on high performance and advanced electronics.
The Panigale V4 R is a homologation special,
designed to be as close to a competition bike as possible for road use.

  • Engine: 1103cc V4
  • Power: ~215 hp
  • Top Speed: 299+ km/h
  • Price (Ex-showroom): ₹27–32 lakh
  • Highlights: Track-focused aerodynamics, advanced electronics,
    Desmosedici-derived engine.

4.suzuki hayabusa

The Suzuki Hayabusa is powered by 1340cc BS6 engine which develops
a power of 190 bhp and a torque of 142 Nm.
With both front and rear disc brakes, Suzuki Hayabusa comes up with
anti-locking braking system.
This Hayabusa bike weighs 266 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 20 liters

  • Engine: 1340cc inline-4
  • Power: ~190 hp
  • Top Speed: 299 km/h (electronically limited)
  • Price: ₹16–17 lakh
  • Highlights: Legendary status, hyper-touring comfort, high-speed stability.
